About Us

Sri Venkateswara Function Hall has been a beacon of tradition, elegance, and joyful celebrations for over a decade. Nestled in a serene and accessible location, our hall blends classical charm with modern luxury, creating a truly divine setting for weddings, receptions, and grand gatherings.

Rooted in heritage and inspired by devotion, our mission is to provide a spiritual yet sophisticated experience to every guest. From intricately designed interiors to state-of-the-art facilities, every element is curated with attention to detail, grace, and grandeur.

Whether you envision a traditional ceremony or a lavish reception, our team ensures every moment is handled with care and perfection — because your celebration deserves nothing less than divine.
